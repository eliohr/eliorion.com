PRE-SAVE CHAPTER I: THE LONG RUN
Eli Orion is releasing a pop rock album in 2024. You can listen to him play around Appleton, WI and Minneapolis, MN. An experienced multi-instrumentalist, composer, and producer, Eli explores a diverse palette of sonic possibilities. There's a lot to listen for in his recordings—lush string sections, blown-out 808s, fingerstyle guitar… Eli asks a lot of questions. He is both humorously sardonic and intimately sincere, with lyrics often introspective but sometimes plainly fun. Influences and inspirations include underscores, Bon Iver, The Microphones, and gabby start.
