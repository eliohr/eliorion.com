CHAPTER III: I'LL BE THERE (THE GOOD PART)
ELI ORION
Multi-instrumentalist, composer, and producer Eli Orion Hooker Reese (local to Minneapolis, MN, and in Appleton, WI) explores a diverse palette of sonic possibilities. There's a lot to listen for in his recordings—lush string sections, blown-out 808s, fingerstyle guitar… Eli is both humorously sardonic and intimately sincere, with lyrics often introspective but sometimes plainly fun. Inspirations include underscores, Bon Iver, The Microphones, and gabby start.

Eli Orion is releasing a pop rock album. Three singles are available now. Three chapters and a fourth single lead up to the album's release on May 17, 2024. “Everything You Didn’t Want to Hear” is a 13-track journey from naivety, through vain self-deceit, and out from the sludge into truth. It is also a collection of all the songs Eli’s had the most fun making yet.

Chapter III pulls us out from dishonesty and darkness with the ethereal Press Rewind, which feels like staring at the shadows of leaves on your wall as you lay on the floor. Sounds inspired by Bon Iver and The 1975’s ambient/electronic works. This one was almost entirely recorded with an iPhone mic with improvised lyrics (the entire song is improvised when it’s played live) and depicts nostalgia for the present. Folk guitar and jazz harmony collide as Deserve warmly reflects on the earliest memories that built me as a person, acknowledges life’s pace picking up, celebrates the whirlwind it is now in my 20s, and surrenders to the infinite power of time. Everything You Didn’t Want to Hear, the title track of the album, brings us back to, “hey life is silly and we get to have fun and reach for meaning while nothing’s really there,” with a contemporarily updated pop-rock feel.
