INFINITY (RIGHT TRACK, PT. 2)
ELI ORION
Multi-instrumentalist, composer, and producer Eli Orion Hooker Reese explores a diverse palette of sonic possibilities. There's a lot to listen for in his recordings—lush string sections, blown-out 808s, fingerstyle guitar…

Eli asks a lot of questions. He is both humorously sardonic and intimately sincere, with lyrics often introspective but sometimes plainly fun. Influences and inspirations include underscores, Bon Iver, The Microphones, and gabby start.

Four singles and three chapters have led up to this album's release. “Everything You Didn’t Want to Hear” is a 13-track journey from naivety, through vain self-deceit, and out from the sludge into truth. It is also a collection of all the songs Eli’s had the most fun making yet.
