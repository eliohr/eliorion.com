I QUIT EVERYTHING
ELI ORION
Multi-instrumentalist, composer, and producer Eli Orion Hooker Reese (local to Minneapolis, MN, and in Appleton, WI) explores a diverse palette of sonic possibilities. There's a lot to listen for in his recordings—lush string sections, blown-out 808s, fingerstyle guitar…

Eli asks a lot of questions. He is both humorously sardonic and intimately sincere, with lyrics often introspective but sometimes plainly fun. Influences and inspirations include underscores, Bon Iver, The Microphones, and gabby start.

Eli Orion is releasing a pop rock album on May 17th 2024. This track, set for July 5th, closes the age of Everything as Eli shifts his focus toward monthly singles for the rest of the year.
