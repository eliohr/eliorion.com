CHAPTER II: PAPER AIRPLANES AND TV PEOPLE
ELI ORION
Multi-instrumentalist, composer, and producer Eli Orion Hooker Reese explores a diverse palette of sonic possibilities. There's a lot to listen for in his recordings—lush string sections, blown-out 808s, fingerstyle guitar… Eli is both humorously sardonic and intimately sincere, with lyrics often introspective but sometimes plainly fun. Inspirations include underscores, Bon Iver, The Microphones, and gabby start.

Eli Orion is releasing a pop rock album. Three singles are available now. Three chapters and a fourth, final single, chronologically populating every two weeks, lead up to the album's release on May 17, 2024. “Everything You Didn’t Want to Hear” is a 13-track journey from naivety, through vain self-deceit, and out from the sludge into truth. It is also a collection of all the songs Eli’s had the most fun making yet.

Chapter II follows Chapter I’s emotional reality-check with a shift in character, telling twisted tales of like love or whatever. Rebound Baby draws on 80s pop and The 1975’s aesthetic (as well as some EDM influences in the bridge). The music video depicts Eli Orion and The Everything playing the song while Xander and Jackson fight over a Culvers’ burger. On The Set Of a Romantic Drama (Are They Filming) is next—a guitar song about truth and love and Eli’s second big The-1975-stylized song. Finally, the lead single of the album, Diesel (I’m Alive) presents many new sounds comparable to Deftones and Jane Remover. It’s a struggle to lift your head from the trudge of life but, when you look up and see the leaves, they’re always prettier than you think. Diesel also has a music video released. Chapter II: Paper Airplanes and TV people comes out April 5, 2024.
